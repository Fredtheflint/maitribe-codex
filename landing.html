What is Mai?

A calm AI companion designed around three dimensions of your wellbeing.

Body

Track your physical energy. Notice patterns. Learn what your body is telling you each day.

Mind

Observe your thoughts without judgment. Create space between stimulus and response.

Soul

Connect with what truly matters to you. Find meaning in the quiet moments between.

How it works

1

Check in

A gentle daily moment. Rate your body, mind, and soul. No pressure, no scores, just honest awareness.

2

Talk to Mai

Share what's on your mind. Mai listens, reflects, and offers one small step forward. Always gentle, never pushy.

3

Grow quietly

Over time, Mai learns your patterns and rhythms. Insights emerge. You begin to see yourself more clearly.

Mai is not a chatbot. Not a coach. Not a therapist. She is a quiet, caring presence — like a thoughtful friend who truly sees you. She acknowledges first, then gently helps you see for yourself.
