## 4. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)
We process personal data for the following purposes:
- **Service delivery** (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): account access, app features, subscriptions.
- **AI feature execution** (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): generating responses, reflections, and companion messages.
- **Security, fraud prevention, and reliability** (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): protecting users and platform integrity.
- **Legal compliance** (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): where required by law.
- **Optional sensitive-like profile inputs** such as birth data are processed based on your clear action to provide them and for feature use (Art. 6(1)(a)/(b) GDPR depending on context).

## 5. AI Processing (Google Gemini API)
For AI-powered functionality, relevant user content (for example chat messages, check-in context, and identity text) is sent to **Google Gemini API** for processing.

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share your data for advertising. Data disclosure is limited to service operation needs, specifically:
- **Google Gemini API** (AI inference)
