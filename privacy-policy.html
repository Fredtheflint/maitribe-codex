MaiTribe Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

We could give you 10 pages of legal language. Instead, we tell you clearly what happens with your data.

1. Who we are

MaiTribe is an AI wellness companion app. Contact: f.stutt@icloud.com.

2. What we store

  • Email address (for login)
  • Name or display name (if you provide it)
  • Check-in scores (Body, Mind, Soul, Energy)
  • Chat messages with Mai
  • Identity text and related reflections
  • Technical settings (language, reminder preferences)

3. Optional data

If you choose astrology features, we may process optional birth data (date, time, place).

4. Where data is stored

Your app data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL). Project setup is intended for EU-region hosting.

5. AI processing (Gemini API)

To generate AI responses, relevant chat/check-in context is sent to Google Gemini API. We do not sell your data. We do not use ad-tracking profiles.

6. Legal basis (GDPR)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) for core app functionality
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) for optional features
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) for app security and reliability

7. Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

8. Your rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Request export (portability)
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing

9. Deletion requests

You can request full account/data deletion by contacting f.stutt@icloud.com.

10. Security

We use access controls, row-level database permissions, and standard transport encryption. No system is perfect, but we design for privacy-first defaults.

11. Health disclaimer

MaiTribe is not medical or psychological treatment and not an emergency service.
