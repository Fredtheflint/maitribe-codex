Privacy, in plain language.

We could give you ten pages of legal text. Instead, here is what actually happens with your data.

What we store

MaiTribe stores your account and app data in Supabase PostgreSQL (EU region). This includes email, profile data, check-ins, identity text and chat history.

What Mai sees

Mai only sees what is needed to respond to you: your chat messages, check-ins and identity context - for your account only.

What goes to third parties

For AI responses, chat content is sent to Google Gemini API. We do not share your data with ad networks or data brokers.

What we do NOT do

No ads. No tracking pixels. No selling your data. No sharing to third parties except Gemini for AI functionality.

Your rights

You can request data export, account deletion, or full data deletion. Write to us and we will handle it.

Contact: f.stutt@icloud.com

Human version

MaiTribe is built to help you - not to exploit attention. Your data exists to support your own reflection and growth, nothing else.
